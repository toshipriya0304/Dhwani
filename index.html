    What makes our Music club unique.
    Versatility

    Be it metal, rock, classical or anything, we respect and entertain all forms of music. We have experts from all fields, specialized at a particular level.

    Workshops

    Events

    Creativity

    About

    Respecting and entertaining all kinds of genre. You say it and we have it.
    Sometimes Music is the only thing that gets Your mind off of Everything else.

    Music is the very essence of our feelings. Whether we are happy, or sad, music acts as the medicine for our soul, by enriching the positive feelings and removing the negative ones. Music club, B.I.T Mesra, is a step towards promotion of music in B.I.T Mesra. It is a place where students display their talents in field of music and expand their knowledge and interest in this field. Throughout the year, Music club is associated with organizing and helping out with management of many exciting and musical events, with active participation in Bitotsav, Pantheon. There are various formal and informal events organized by music club, which include Talent Hunt, Live Jams, and Battle of Bands. Apart from organizing events, Music club also helps students to enrich their hobbies and improve their talents by providing workshops in various fields of music. Music club is a platform to explore yourself through music. It is a place where musical talents of B.I.T Mesra collide to create something beautiful.
